About Xtra Ozzed

Xtra Ozzed is an innovative electronic music producer and DJ known for pushing the boundaries of sound. With a unique blend of atmospheric textures and driving beats, Xtra Ozzed creates immersive sonic experiences that transport listeners to other worlds.


Having released music on renowned label TUNECAT, Xtra Ozzed has garnered support from industry heavyweights and built a dedicated following across streaming platforms and club circuits worldwide.


The upcoming single "PROPHECY" represents a new chapter in the artist's journey, exploring darker tones and more complex arrangements while maintaining the signature sound that fans have come to love.
